Crime

Public Consumption Tickets are non-criminal citations issued for the consumption of marijuana in a public place.

Industry Related Crimes are crimes related to the licensed marijuana industry and are committed against the industry or by the industry itself.

Non-Industry Related Crimes are crimes in which Marijuana is the primary target in the commission of these crimes but the marijuana has no readily apparent tie to a licensed operation.

Incidentally Related Crimes are crimes which involve possession and consumption of marijuana during another crime by offender or victim or both.
